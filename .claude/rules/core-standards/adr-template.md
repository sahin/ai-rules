# ADR Template (MANDATORY FORMAT)

Use this exact template for all Architecture Decision Records:

```markdown
# ADR-[NUMBER]: [TITLE]

## Status
[Proposed | Accepted | Deprecated | Superseded by ADR-XXX]

## Date
[YYYY-MM-DD when decision was made]

## Context
What issue are we addressing? What factors drive this decision?

### Background
- Current situation
- Problems with existing approach
- Business/technical drivers
- Operating constraints

### Requirements
- Functional requirements
- Non-functional requirements (performance, security)
- Compliance requirements
- Business constraints (budget, timeline)

## Decision
What change are we proposing and why?

### Chosen Solution
[Detailed description of selected approach]

### Key Factors
- Technical factors
- Business factors
- Risk factors

## Alternatives Considered

### Option 1: [Name]
- Description
- Pros
- Cons
- Why rejected

### Option 2: [Name]
- Description
- Pros
- Cons
- Why rejected

## Consequences

### Positive
- [List positive outcomes]

### Negative
- [List negative impacts]

### Risks
- [List risks and mitigation]

## Implementation
- Migration strategy
- Timeline
- Team assignments
- Success metrics

## References
- [Links to relevant docs, RFCs, issues]
```
